About the Artist

Taylor German

Owner & tattoo artist at Diamond in the Rogue Tattoo and Arts, Grants Pass, Oregon.

Portrait of Taylor German
The Person Behind the Needle

Building A Space That Feels Safe First

Taylor German opened Diamond in the Rogue in 2023 with a simple standard: every client should feel comfortable, heard, and welcome — no exceptions. It's a female-owned studio, and that shows up in how the space is run: calm, clean, unhurried, and judgment-free.

Taylor works across most tattoo styles, from delicate fine line and florals to bold color and black & grey — always starting with a real conversation about what the piece means to you before a single line goes down.

"Shine without apology."

The Approach

What To Expect

Real Conversation First

Every piece starts with talking through your idea, placement, and what it means to you — not a rushed walk-in sketch.

A Genuinely Safe Space

Diamond in the Rogue is female-owned and built to be a place anyone can walk into and feel comfortable, whatever your first tattoo story is.

Open to Most Styles

Fine line, color, black & grey, florals, custom lettering and more — one artist, a wide range of work.
