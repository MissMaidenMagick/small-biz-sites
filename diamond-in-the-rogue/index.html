A Shop Built On Feeling Safe

Diamond in the Rogue is a female-owned studio run by artist Taylor German. Clients consistently describe it as the most comfortable shop they've been in — clean, calming, and judgment-free from the moment you walk through the door.

Whether it's your first tattoo or your fiftieth, the goal is the same: listen first, design something you'll love, and make the whole process feel good.
