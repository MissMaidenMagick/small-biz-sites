What Is DiamondVIBE?

Sound & Vibration, In One Space

The In Harmony bed combines gentle sound frequencies with full-body vibration to help you unwind — a calming complement to the studio's tattoo work, or a relaxation session on its own.

Many clients use a session before or after tattoo work to ease nerves and settle in; others book it purely as a standalone relaxation experience.
