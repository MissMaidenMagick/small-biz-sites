Our Story

Brother & Sister-Owned, Community Built

How We Started

A little pine, standing alone — until it wasn't

The Lonely Pine started with a simple idea: build the kind of coffee shop we'd want to spend our own afternoons in. Slow mornings, honest ingredients, and a counter where you're greeted by name after the second visit.

What began as a small storefront in The Hive Building has grown into a gathering spot for downtown Grants Pass — regulars working from the corner table, kids coloring while parents catch up, and neighbors trading stories over a cardamom rose latte.
