Our Story

Craft coffee, kept close to home

The Lonely Pine is a brother and sister-owned café built around a simple idea: good coffee tastes better shared. From cardamom rose lattes to vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options for everyone at the table, we built a space that feels like home — cozy corners for remote work, room for the kids to play, and a counter full of regulars who've become family.
